Cookie Policy

Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to understand how our website is being used.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us to remember your preferences and to understand how you interact with our website. Cookies can also be used to personalize your experience on our website, such as by remembering your login information.

Types of Cookies Used on Our Website

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are used to remember your preferences during your current visit to our website and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies are used to remember your preferences for future visits to our website and are stored on your device for a longer period of time.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are placed by third-party services that we use on our website, such as Google Analytics and social media widgets.

Managing Cookies

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but doing so may affect the functionality of our website. You can also use browser extensions or add-ons to block or delete cookies.
